full stack developer

Paul always had an interest in computer technologies since a young age, but due to living in a remote outback location, he never got to undertake any early childhood studies. This would change later in his early teenage years after moving to a more metropolitan area in the Northern Territory and out of necessity to create websites for his own businesses as a young entrepreneur.

Paul began self-learning the most basic language, building websites in pure HTML using Microsoft Frontpage and Adobe Dreamweaver. At this stage, CSS did not exist.

Paul then discovered Content Management Systems and started developing websites using Joomla 1.0 and then (many years later) ventured into the world of Wordpress.
