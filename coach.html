strength coach

Paul is a Personal Trainer and Strength Coach with 15 years experience in the health and fitness industry. Paul started out in the health and fitness industry in 2004 and has since built a career in Melbourne as a one on one appointment based Strength Coach, mostly working out of Fitness First gyms in the CBD.

Paul also teaches Group Fitness classes at Fitness First with up to as many as 70 to 80 participants at any given time.

Senior Coach @ Paul Meier Fitness

Paul has owned and operated his own business 'Paul Meier Fitness' since entering into the health and fitness industry, which mostly operates out of Fitness First gyms in Melbourne's CBD. Paul has also mentored and coached several junior Personal Trainers over the years, helping them to become successful in their own right in the health and fitness industry.
